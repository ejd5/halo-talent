# WTF — Where Talent Forms : Growth Tracking & Analytics Report

This document reports on the analytics tracking infrastructure set up for WTF — Where Talent Forms.

## 1. Tracked Events and Objectives
To measure organic and lead generation performance, we monitor:
- **CTA Conversions**: Clicks on "Candidater" (`/apply`) and "Réserver une démo" (`/demo`).
- **Resource Downloads**: Click events on resource cards under `/ressources-gratuites`.
- **Blog Interactions**: Scroll depth and reading engagement metrics.
- **Form Submissions**: Contact inquiries and application forms.

## 2. Privacy & GDPR Integrity
- All tracking respects user privacy.
- No personal identifiable information (PII) is captured during click tracking.
- Complies with European GDPR rules by providing an explicit disclaimer on all download routes.
