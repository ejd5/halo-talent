# WTF — Where Talent Forms : Ethical Backlink & PR Strategy Plan

This document details the ethical backlink and PR outreach strategy to build domain authority without spammy methods.

## 1. Targeted Sites for Outreach
We target highly authoritative, contextually relevant platforms:
- **Niche Creators & Podcasts**: Interviews or joint sessions about safety, image rights, and business workflows.
- **Creative Studios & Agencies**: Collaborative articles with professional photography, videography, or audio engineering services.
- **Creator Economy Newsletters**: Feature articles focusing on transparent revenue distribution (e.g. WTF commissions).
- **Tool Reviews**: Submitting CRM/Atlas configurations to SaaS comparison sites.

## 2. 5 Email Outreach Templates
We have drafted 5 personalized, non-spammy outreach templates for:
1. **Photographer Collaboration**: Offering to link to their portfolio in exchange for co-authoring a photography branding guide.
2. **Podcast Guest Pitch**: Requesting a slot to discuss "creator safety and legal prep".
3. **Business/Marketing Blogger**: Proposing a guest article on "Commission Agence Créateur".
4. **Fitness Coach**: Proposing a study on "Gym branding & fitness sponsorship contracts".
5. **Music Producer**: Pitching a guest piece about "Independent music copyrights and Fanbase CRM".

## 3. Strict Compliance Rules
- **No Mass Submissions**: Only highly targeted, personal outreach.
- **Zero Paid Link Networks**: Avoid paying for link placements.
- **Natural Anchor Text**: Use natural phrases rather than stuffed keywords.
- **No PBNs (Private Blog Networks)**: Rely on real brand referrals.
