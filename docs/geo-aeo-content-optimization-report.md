# WTF — Where Talent Forms : GEO/AEO Content Optimization Report

This report presents our strategy to ensure WTF is easily crawlable and rankable by Large Language Model based search engines (ChatGPT/SearchGPT, Claude, Gemini).

## 1. Structured Visual Formatting
LLM search assistants prioritize structured content. To optimize for these engines, all pillar pages and articles feature:
- **"À retenir" Callout Cards**: Highlights key takeaways in high-contrast sections.
- **Comparison Grids**: Uses standard Markdown tables (compiled into semantic `<table />` HTML tags) that are highly readable for tokenizers.
- **Interactive FAQs**: Employs `<details>` and `<summary>` components to provide direct answers to common queries, which helps Google search pull relevant Answer Box summaries.

## 2. Definitive Wording and Fact Citations
- **Definitions**: Direct definitions of marketing and legal terms are integrated across the glossary and article intros.
- **Reliable Data References**: References are made to general market observations (e.g. creator economy statistics) to build topical authority without inventing figures.
- **Author/Expertise Signals (E-E-A-T)**: Clear editorial attribution (Madison, Camille, Niran, Alessio, Rafael) is added to demonstrate expertise and authoritativeness.
