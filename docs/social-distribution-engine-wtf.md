# WTF — Where Talent Forms : Social Distribution Engine

This document details the playbook to repurpose and distribute every long-form blog article into a suite of social media assets.

## 1. Content Recycling Workflow
For every published article, we generate 7 social assets:
1. **LinkedIn Post**: Strategic breakdown for founders and creator partners.
2. **X (Twitter) Thread**: Concise points discussing "problem -> error -> WTF solution".
3. **Instagram Carousel Script**: Outline for ivory-and-gold visual slides.
4. **3 TikTok/Reels Scripts**: Hook, screen text, and CTA guidelines.
5. **Instagram Story Mockup**: A snippet with a swipe-up link to read the article.
6. **Newsletter Digest**: Short teaser sent to the subscriber base.
7. **Visual Quote**: Extracting an "À retenir" sentence to share as an image.

## 2. 30-Day Content Distribution Calendar
A pre-configured 30-day posting matrix scheduling our 30 new long-tail articles across channels (LinkedIn, X, Instagram, TikTok) pointing back to relevant tools (Atlas, Lex, Protection, Commissions) is included to orchestrate continuous growth.
