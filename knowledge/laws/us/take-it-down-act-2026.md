## Overview

The "TAKE IT DOWN Act" criminalizes the publication of non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII), including AI-generated deepfakes. Platforms must remove such content within 48 hours of notification.

## Key Provisions

- Creation AND distribution of NCII deepfakes = federal crime
- Platforms must have clear reporting mechanisms
- Victims can sue for damages (statutory minimum $25,000)
- No immunity under Section 230 for NCII content

## Implications

- Tout contenu IA généré sans consentement = crime fédéral US
- Les agences doivent avoir des procédures de signalement
- Les créateurs US sont protégés même si l'agence est basée ailleurs
